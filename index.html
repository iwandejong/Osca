Welcome to Osca

Tired of outdated Wiki's?

Then this is the place for you. I created a framework for developers that eases the process of documenting your framework or language.

100% Customizable. 100% Free. 100% Yours.

About Osca

Welcome to the Osca community! Here is a bit of fanformation ;)

Osca started in 2021 as an initiative with self-taught coder and matriculant, Iwan de Jong, to provide a modern Wiki backbone for any developer.

Osca is currently striving to ease the process of coding. Translate your website into a minimal and focused website for users to access easily.

Intuitive UI
All your frameworks in one place
Focused on the important information
Open Source
Feedback-based community
Downloadable Software (coming soon!)

Osca is currently using Github as a main source for contributions and feedback, but more features are on the horizon and you can keep up-to-date by subscribing to our newsletter or by following Osca on Github.

Happy coding!
